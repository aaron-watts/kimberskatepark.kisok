Safety Information

Kimber Skatepark strongly advises users to wear helmets!

Helmets are available for hire for free - no deposit needed
Ramps are dangerous when wet! Use at your own risk! We do not lend out hire bikes, scooters or skateboards when it is raining or wet!
Any users under 8 years old MUST be supervised at all times.

Skatepark Etiquette

Wait your turn!

Don't go when the way is not clear.
Follow the lines!

Don't ride in strange directions unless you know it is safe to do so. It is hard to for other users to predict where you are going and can cause accidents. If you are unsure what the expected routes are on the ramps, then watch the more experienced users to learn from them.
Look before you go!

Always check it is clear before you begin riding on the park.
